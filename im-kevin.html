Hey Neighbor

Hey Neighbor,

I’m Kevin Deneault. I care about where Olathe is headed—and how we get there together.

To me, this means a purposeful vision for the future, building connections between people, and maintaining amazing services.

I believe in planning smart growth—where new development and businesses add value to our neighborhoods, jobs to our economy, and life to our public spaces.

It’s important to me to create a more connected community, where people know their neighbors, have more opportunities to interact and move within our city easily. 

I'll be a good steward of Olathe’s high-quality services and public safety—making sure they remain strong, reliable, and accessible to everyone.

I’m here to listen to you, to be practical, and to focus on what actually improves life for the people who live here in Olathe.
